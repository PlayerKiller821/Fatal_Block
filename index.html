服务器信息 / Server Info

Fatal_Block is a Minecraft Bedrock Edition multiplayer survival server, founded on August 26th, 2023. Its goal is to create a pure multiplayer survival experience, with an open policy towards the vast majority of player actions. It allows PVP and free-for-all combat, supports Xbox Achievements, and features minimal modifications to the vanilla survival gameplay.

Fatal_Block是一个Minecraft基岩版多人生存服务器，创建于2023年8月26日，其目标是打造一个纯净的多人生存服务器，且对玩家的绝大部分行为持开放态度，允许PVP和自由混战，支持Xbox成就，对原版游戏生存模式几乎没有修改。
